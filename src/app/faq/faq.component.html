FAQ

Why do I need an account?

So you can track which tours you have completed, work your way towards badges, and enjoy the full Explor’d experience.

 Why does Explor’d need access to my location?

We use your location to determine which tours are closest to you, and to provide access to audio tours when you are close enough.

 Why can’t I access tour audio without visiting the location?

Because in order to have the full experience, we reckon you should visit a place to experience it! Once you are within 200 metres of the nominated location, you will be able to listen to the tour

 What are badges?

Badges are a reward for completing tours. Complete a nominated set of tours (usually based around a theme) and you will receive a badge! Sometimes these badges will come with a reward for completion. See if you can get them all! Note: badges may be added, removed or modified at any time without notice.

 Why aren’t there any tours near me?

Because Explor’d is only just starting out! We’re trying to get as many high quality tours out there as we can, but this will take time. Keep an eye out – we are releasing more audio tours every month! If you know a place that absolutely MUST have an Explor’d site, get in contact!

 Is Explor’d available anywhere other than Australia?

At this point, Explor’d tours are only available in Australia – we hope to change this in the future!

 I have suggestions for improving the app, or want to partner for an Explor’d site. How can I get in contact?

We would love to hear from you! Use the contact us button within the app, or email us: info@explord.com.au
